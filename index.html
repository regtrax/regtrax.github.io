China's Crypto Ban

Submitted By: CodeX Member 54 Categories: Asia, China Schedule: Released: April 23 | Public Discussion: May 01-May 08 | Report: May 20

China's ban on cryptocurrencies has been in effect since late 2016. This brought huge economic ramifications both locally and worldwide. This week, we discuss this ban, and how we think China may move forward in the future in light of recent policy developments from other Asian nations.
