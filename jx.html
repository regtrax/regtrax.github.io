Key Jurisdictions

United States

Despite being a global tech leader, the U.S. has been surprisingly slow to offer regulatory guidance to blockchain projects. As a web2.0 powerhouse, the U.S. policy on blockchain will serve to influence regulations across much of the rest of the world.

Explore

Japan

Japan has been vocal about intending to support the blockchain ecosystem, a stance that has helped them grow into one of the largest markets in the world. Their treatment of "virtual currencies" offers regulatory clarity well beyond most jurisdictions.

Explore

Switzerland

Switzerland has become a hub for blockchain innovation in large part because of the regulatory certainty that exists here. Switzerland is also home to Crypto Valley, a small but dense pocket of blockchain companies, start-ups, and foundations.
